Syntax
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
pdf-extract extract --bodies <PDF-File>

Options:
    -- characters: outputs characters per page.
    -- chunks: outputs chunks (=lines?) per page, but unsorted.
    -- regions: outputs lines per region and page (*)
    -- columns: outputs the coordinates (but no textual content) of columns per page, 
    -- titles: outputs the title
    -- sections: outputs lines per section and page (*)
    -- bodies: outputs the coordinates (but no textual content) of bodies(?),
